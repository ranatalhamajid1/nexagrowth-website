E-Commerce · Paid Ads

LuxeVibe Fashion: From Low ROAS to 280% Growth

The Problem

LuxeVibe Fashion, an online clothing brand based in Lahore, was spending PKR 50,000/month on Facebook Ads but only generating PKR 70,000 in revenue — a ROAS of just 1.4x. Their campaigns were poorly structured: they were boosting posts instead of running proper campaigns, targeting all of Pakistan with no audience segmentation, and using low-quality product photos.

Our Strategy

  • Campaign restructuring: Migrated from boosted posts to proper Ads Manager campaigns with clear objectives (Conversions > Purchase)
  • Audience research: Created segmented audiences by city (Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad) and interest groups (fashion, online shopping, Daraz users)
  • Creative overhaul: Shot new lifestyle product photos and created short video ads with Urdu captions
  • Retargeting funnel: Set up website visitors retargeting and abandoned cart campaigns
  • A/B testing: Ran 3-4 ad variations per ad set to find winning creatives

Local Business · SEO + Social

Karachi Restaurant Chain: 45% More Walk-Ins in 60 Days

The Problem

A popular restaurant with 3 locations in Karachi was struggling with low foot traffic despite great food and competitive pricing. They had no Google My Business profile, their social media was inconsistent, and they had zero online reviews. People simply couldn't find them when searching "restaurants near me" on Google.

Our Strategy

  • Google My Business: Created and fully optimized GMB profiles for all 3 locations with photos, menu, hours, and descriptions
  • Review generation: Implemented a review request system — QR codes on tables linking to Google Reviews
  • Localized Facebook/Instagram Ads: Targeted people within 5km radius of each location with food photography and limited-time offers
  • Influencer partnerships: Invited 3 local food bloggers for complimentary meals in exchange for honest reviews
  • Content calendar: Consistent posting schedule — 4 posts/week showcasing dishes, behind-the-scenes, and customer moments

SaaS · SEO + Content

NexaBuild SaaS: From Zero Organic Traffic to Page 1 Rankings

The Problem

NexaBuild, a project management SaaS tool targeting Pakistani freelancers and agencies, had zero organic traffic. Their entire customer acquisition was through paid ads at PKR 800 per lead — unsustainable for a startup. Their website had no blog, poor page speed, and critical technical SEO issues preventing Google from even indexing most pages.

Our Strategy

  • Technical SEO audit: Fixed indexing issues, improved page speed from 4.2s to 1.8s, added proper meta tags and structured data
  • Keyword research: Identified 50+ low-competition, high-intent keywords relevant to Pakistani freelancers and agencies
  • Content strategy: Published 2 in-depth blog posts per week targeting identified keywords — guides, comparisons, and how-to articles
  • Internal linking: Built a strategic internal linking structure connecting blog posts to product pages
  • Backlink outreach: Secured guest posts on 8 relevant Pakistani tech and business blogs
