📖 How to Use This Tool

The Word Counter analyzes your text instantly as you type, giving you detailed statistics without needing to press any buttons.

  • Type or Paste: Enter your text in the input area above. All counts update in real-time.
  • Words: Total number of words separated by whitespace.
  • Characters: Total characters including spaces, and a separate count excluding spaces.
  • Sentences: Counted by periods, exclamation marks, and question marks.
  • Paragraphs: Counted by non-empty lines separated by line breaks.
  • Reading Time: Estimated at 200 words per minute, the average adult reading speed.
  • Copy or Reset: Use the buttons to copy your text or clear the input.

This tool is perfect for writers, students, bloggers, and anyone who needs to check word count for essays, social media posts, or SEO content.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How does the word counter work?
Simply type or paste your text into the input area. The tool counts words, characters, sentences, paragraphs, and reading time in real-time as you type — no button clicks needed.
What is the difference between characters with and without spaces?
Characters with spaces counts every character including spaces and line breaks. Characters without spaces excludes all whitespace, giving you only the count of actual letters, numbers, and symbols.
How is reading time estimated?
Reading time is estimated based on an average reading speed of 200 words per minute, which is the standard for adult readers.
Is there a text length limit?
No. The tool runs entirely in your browser, so there is no upload or server limit. It can handle very large texts with ease.
