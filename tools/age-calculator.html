📖 How to Use This Tool

The Age Calculator makes it easy to determine your exact age and discover fun facts about your birthday:

  • Select your date of birth — Use the date picker to choose your birth date.
  • Optionally change the "As of" date — Defaults to today, but you can set any target date to calculate age at that point.
  • Click "Calculate Age" — Your exact age in years, months, and days appears along with detailed stats.
  • Explore your results — See total days lived, hours lived, birthday countdown, estimated heartbeats, and your zodiac sign.
  • Share your results — Use the Share button to share this tool with friends.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How does the age calculator work?
The calculator takes your date of birth and computes the exact difference between that date and today (or your chosen reference date). It breaks the difference down into years, months, and remaining days, accounting for varying month lengths.
Does it account for leap years?
Yes, the calculator fully accounts for leap years when computing your total days lived and exact age. February 29th birthdays are handled correctly.
How is the zodiac sign determined?
Your zodiac sign is determined by the month and day of your birth falling within the standard Western astrological date ranges for each of the 12 zodiac signs.
Can I calculate age between two dates?
Yes! Use the "As of Date" field to set a custom end date. This lets you calculate the age or duration between any two dates — not just from birth to today.
Is the estimated heartbeat count accurate?
The heartbeat estimate is based on an average resting heart rate of ~72 beats per minute. Actual heartbeats vary based on activity level, fitness, age, and health conditions. It's a fun approximation!
