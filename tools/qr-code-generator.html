❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is a QR code?
A QR (Quick Response) code is a two-dimensional barcode that stores data like URLs, text, or contact information. It can be scanned instantly by any smartphone camera, making it a powerful tool for bridging physical and digital experiences.
Can I customize the QR code colors?
Yes! You can set custom foreground (QR pattern) and background colors using the color pickers. Ensure there's enough contrast between colors for reliable scanning.
Are the QR codes free for commercial use?
Absolutely! All QR codes generated are free for both personal and commercial use. Print them on business cards, packaging, posters, menus — anywhere you need them.
Do the QR codes expire?
No! QR codes generated by our tool are static — the data is encoded directly into the image. They never expire and will always work as long as the encoded URL or data is valid.
